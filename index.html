About Gwalior City

Gwalior, located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, is a historic and culturally vibrant city known for its majestic forts, classical music heritage, and architectural marvels. Often referred to as the “Cultural Capital of Madhya Pradesh,” the city is a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Gwalior is home to the renowned Gwalior Gharana, one of the oldest schools of Hindustani classical music, and has produced legendary artists over centuries. The city’s skyline is dominated by the iconic Gwalior Fort, a grand structure that stands as a testament to its royal past and strategic importance.

Beyond history and music, Gwalior is also emerging as a growing hub for education, research, and innovation, with several reputed institutions and industries. Its strategic location and excellent connectivity via road, rail, and air make it an ideal destination for national and international events.

Nearby attractions such as Mitawali, Padhawali, Kakanmath, and Sankua Dham offer visitors a glimpse into the region’s rich spiritual and architectural legacy. With its harmonious mix of history, culture, and progress, Gwalior welcomes all with warmth and inspiration.

About SRCEM

Established in 1998, ShriRam College of Engineering & Management (SRCEM) has emerged as a center of excellence in technical and professional education in Central India. The institute is committed to academic rigor, innovation, and industrial relevance. Located in the serene surroundings of Banmore, near the historic city of Gwalior, the campus spans over 50 acres, offering an ideal environment for focused learning and all-round development.

SRCEM is approved by AICTE and affiliated with RGPV, Bhopal. The college offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and applied sciences. Its industry-aligned curriculum, modern laboratories, and ICT-enabled classrooms ensure students receive hands-on experience and practical exposure.

With its motto of **“Knowledge is Power,”** SRCEM continues to inspire innovation, excellence, and leadership in every student.

About PRAA Robotics & Research Lab

The **PRAA Robotic Research & Development Labs** is a hub of technological excellence, committed to pushing the boundaries of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. Our core belief is that innovation thrives in a collaborative ecosystem, where academia, industry, and society intersect.

We conduct cutting-edge research in domains such as robotic automation, machine learning algorithms, natural language understanding, computer vision, and AI applications in healthcare, agriculture, and education. Our research not only aims for technical breakthroughs but also focuses on ethical implications and societal benefits of AI.

By nurturing a culture of curiosity, inclusiveness, and purpose-driven innovation, PRAA Labs is determined to lead the charge in shaping an AI-enabled future that is sustainable, inclusive, and beneficial for all.
